# Trade-offs and follow-ups (AI systems)

**Trade-offs:** batch vs online inference; **GPU** utilization vs latency; **central** model hub vs **embedded** endpoints; **open models** vs **hosted API**.

**Follow-ups:** cold start for batch; **A/B** model routing; **fairness** and **bias** testing; **copyright** / training data risk; **DR** when region loses GPU quota.

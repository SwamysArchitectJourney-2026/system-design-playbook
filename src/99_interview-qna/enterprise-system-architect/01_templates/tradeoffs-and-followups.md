# Trade-offs and follow-ups (enterprise architect)

**Trade-offs:** **hub-and-spoke** integration vs **point-to-point**; **ESB** nostalgia vs **event + API** modern; **custom** vs **COTS**.

**Follow-ups:** **SLA** between business units; **data ownership** disputes; **audit** trail for cross-border data; **kill switch** for failing partner integration.
